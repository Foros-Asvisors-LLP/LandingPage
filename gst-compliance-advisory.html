GST Compliance and Advisors

Expert Advisors for GST compliance and advisors in India and overseas.

Are you a foreign company doing business in India? Or an Indian business with cross-border operations? Navigating GST compliance in India can be complex – but you don't have to do it alone. At Foros Advisors LLP, we specialise in providing tailored GST compliance services in India for foreign entities and Indian businesses. From registration to filing, advisory to audits – we ensure your GST obligations are handled professionally and efficiently.

  • Correct classification of goods and services
  • Timely GST return filing
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) reconciliation
  • Handling import-export transactions under GST
  • Navigating audits and assessments

Without expert support, non-compliance can lead to penalties, delays, or tax inefficiencies.

1. GST Registration in India

Whether you're a foreign entity or a growing Indian business, we help you register under GST quickly and correctly – including online GST registration in Delhi and PAN India.

2. GST Return Filing & Compliance

We manage timely and accurate monthly, quarterly, and annual GST filings, helping you avoid late fees and interest.

3. Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management

Our experts ensure correct ITC claims, proper documentation, and reconciliation to minimise your tax liability.

4. GST Advisory & Transaction Structuring

Receive strategic guidance on complex transactions, cross-border GST applicability, place of supply, and tax optimisation.

5. GST Refunds for Exporters

We help you claim GST refunds efficiently, especially in zero-rated export transactions and inverted duty structures.

6. GST Audit & Health Checks

Be proactive with your compliance. Our GST audits and reviews detect errors before they become costly.

7. Litigation & Representation

Facing notices or scrutiny? Our experts represent you before GST authorities and ensure your case is presented effectively.
