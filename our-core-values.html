Our Core Principles

These values define who we are and how we serve our clients every day.

Excellence

We seek candidates with outstanding intellectual capacity and the highest ethical standards. We apply this rigor to every analysis and recommendation.

Client Focus

We bank individual companies rather than industries, pursuing long-term value over short-term transactions. Our success is measured by yours.

Independence

We provide truly independent advice, free from the conflicts of lending or activist relationships. Our guidance is completely objective.

Respect & Teamwork

We foster a culture of mutual respect and collaboration. A diverse team, working with shared purpose, brings the best ideas to the table.

Hard Work & Fun

High-stakes advisory requires intense dedication, but we maintain a positive, energetic environment where our team can thrive.

Diversity

We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better creative solutions for seemingly intractable challenges.

We are dedicated to maintaining these standards across every engagement.
