Our Core Principles

These values define who we are and how we serve our clients every day.

  • Excellence. We seek candidates who possess outstanding intellectual capacity, the highest ethical standards, and a deep commitment to excellence. We apply this rigor to every analysis and recommendation we provide.
  • Client focus. We bank individual companies rather than industries, and we pursue long-term value versus short-term transactions. Our success is measured solely by the success of our clients.
  • Independence. We provide truly independent advice. We are not a lender to or customer of our clients, and we do not work for activist investors. This allows us to offer completely objective guidance.
  • Respect, Teamwork and Diversity. We foster a culture of mutual respect and collaboration. We believe that a diverse team, working together with shared purpose, brings the best ideas to the table.
  • Hard work and fun. High-stakes advisory requires intense dedication, but we believe in maintaining a positive, energetic environment where our team can thrive.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to maintaining these standards across every engagement.
