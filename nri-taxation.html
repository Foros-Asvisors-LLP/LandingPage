NRI Taxation Services in India

Expert advisory for NRI Taxation Services in India and overseas.

Are you a Non-Resident Indian (NRI) looking for expert guidance on Indian taxes? At Foros Advisors LLP, we offer comprehensive and reliable NRI taxation services in India tailored to your unique financial needs. As one of the most trusted NRI tax consultants in India, we bring years of experience, deep domain knowledge, and a client-first approach to help NRIs across the globe navigate Indian tax laws with confidence. Whether it's income tax, capital gains, investments, or property matters, we ensure full compliance while helping you save money and avoid legal hassles.

Understanding your residential status is the first step in determining your tax liability in India. According to the Indian Income Tax Act, an individual qualifies as an NRI if:

  • They spend more than 182 days outside India in a financial year, or
  • They stay in India for less than 60 days during the financial year (with certain exceptions).

This status must be reviewed annually. Our team helps you assess and document your status to ensure proper NRI tax filing services in India.

2. Determination of Residential Status

We evaluate your travel history, personal ties, and economic connections to accurately determine your residency status under Section 6 of the Income Tax Act.

We help you avoid double taxation through the right Double Tax Avoidance Agreement (DTAA) benefits and assist in obtaining the Tax Residency Certificate (TRC).

5. Property Transactions & Capital Gains

6. Handling Tax Notices & Appeals

Received a notice under Section 142(1), 148, or 148A? Our experienced team handles responses, representations, and appeals, ensuring swift resolution of tax issues.

7. PAN & NRO/NRE Bank Account Assistance
